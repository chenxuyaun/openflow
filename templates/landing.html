The project drifts across long threads, hidden context, and fragile summaries. A fresh restart often means rebuilding the state by hand.

What You See First

1. A clear goal

2. Saved materials

You see the files, notes, and inputs the work should rely on.

3. Current progress

You can tell what is happening now and what has already been done.

4. A suggested next step

You get a clear recommendation for what should happen next.
