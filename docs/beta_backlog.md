# OpenFlow Beta Backlog

This document captures work that is intentionally out of Alpha scope.

## Product Expansion

- Stronger project-type-specific workspace layouts for research, writing, planning, and delivery
- More opinionated landing storytelling, richer first-run demo states, and higher-polish motion and visual design
- Better guidance for non-technical users when choosing collaboration style and review depth

## Governance Depth

- Full decision history with change log, actor attribution, and richer audit trails
- More configurable governance gates, including multi-step approval policies
- Deeper handoff comparison and review diff views

## Knowledge Operations

- More powerful research pack parsing, normalization, and automatic linking
- Better search, filtering, and grouping across large project material sets
- Stronger file indexing and retrieval aids beyond the Alpha baseline

## Workflow Intelligence

- More adaptive role generation by domain and project stage
- Smarter next-step recommendations from project state and decision evidence
- Deeper replanning behavior after blocked reviews or shifting goals

## Delivery Surface

- Export and packaging flows for reports, plans, and stakeholder-ready summaries
- Multi-user collaboration patterns and realtime coordination features
- Stronger release packaging and deployment presets
